FAQs

How does the typing test work?


When you start typing, the 1-minute timer begins. Your goal is to type as much of the given paragraph as possible before time runs out. The test tracks your speed (WPM, CPM) and accuracy in real-time.

How do I restart the test with a new paragraph?


Click the Restart button to instantly reset your stats and load a new random paragraph. This allows you to start fresh and try improving your typing speed and accuracy with different text each time.

What do WPM (words per minute) and CPM (characters per minute) mean?


WPM measures how many words you type in one minute, assuming 5 characters per word. CPM counts the total characters typed per minute. WPM is an approximate value, while CPM gives a more exact character-based speed.

How can I improve my typing speed?


Practice regularly, focus on accuracy before speed, and try not to look at the keyboard. Using all fingers (touch typing) and keeping a consistent rhythm can greatly improve both speed and accuracy over time.
