Before you begin!

As soon as you start typing, the 1-minute timer begins. Your goal is to type as much of the paragraph as possible — ideally the whole thing — before time runs out. While you type, you’ll see your WPM (words per minute), CPM (characters per minute), and Mistakes counters update in real time. WPM is only an approximate number, not an exact measurement. If you click the Restart button, all stats will reset and a brand-new paragraph will be loaded. Once the minute is over, typing will be disabled.
-Good luck, and happy typing!
